Gas producing Stovetop

No more buying stored gas cylinders. Stovetop generates cooking gas on demand.

Cooking gas on demand

Using water and electricity, Caspo produces cooking gas as and when you want it, at a cost on par with LPG, and in some cases even lesser.

LPG hybrid

Caspo has the unique feature of a Hybrid Burner which conveniently switches between LPG and self-produced cooking gas as per demand. During normal usage, Caspo itself uses a perfect mixture of both kind of gas. This helps in generating maximum cost saving based on your electricity and LPG rates. It is also possible to cook with either of the gases independently.

Cook smart, not hard

Control your stovetop remotely using your mobile phone or tablet from anywhere within your home.

Phone connected learning stovetop

This Internet of Things stovetop couples with your phone or tablet. The software and hardware works in synergy to learn your cooking habits and pattern and helps you to multitask smartly. Now you can work on your office presentation without worrying about what is on your stovetop.
