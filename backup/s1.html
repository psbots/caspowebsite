LPG hybrid

Caspo has the unique feature of a Hybrid Burner which conveniently switches between LPG and “self- made” cooking gas as per demand. During normal usage, Caspo itself uses a perfect mixture of both kind of gas. This helps in generating maximum cost saving based on your electricity and LPG rates. It is also possible to cook with either of the gases independently.
